Intima Account and Data Deletion

Last updated: June 24, 2026

Developer: Dimitar Boychev · dimitur.boychew@abv.bg

This page explains how Intima users can delete their account and associated data. It applies to the Intima mobile app for Android.

Delete Local Data on Your Device

You can delete local Intima data directly in the app:

  1. Open Intima.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Open the Data section.
  4. Tap Delete everything and confirm.

This deletes local diary and calendar data, local media files, local app settings, and local security keys from your device. If server copies of diary photos or videos exist, the app also requests deletion of those server copies.

Request Server-Side Account and Data Deletion

To request deletion of server-side account data, email us:

dimitur.boychew@abv.bg

Use the subject line Intima account deletion request.

Include the following information if you have it:

  • The email address used for Google or Facebook sign-in, if you used one.
  • Whether you used Partner Mode.
  • Approximate dates of use, if you only used anonymous Partner Mode.
  • Any details that help us identify the correct account, without sending private diary text or sensitive photos.

What We Delete

After verifying the request, we delete or anonymize server-side data linked to the account where technically possible, including:

  • Supabase authentication account records used by Intima.
  • Partner Mode pairing/couple records and chat records.
  • Partner Mode media stored on our backend.
  • Private server copies of diary photos or videos.
  • Couple Match pose-interest records.

What May Be Kept Temporarily

Some data may remain for a limited period where required for security, abuse prevention, troubleshooting, legal compliance, or backup recovery. Backups and logs are retained only as long as reasonably necessary and are not used for normal app functionality after deletion processing.

Processing Time

We aim to process deletion requests within 30 days. If we need more information to identify the account or verify the request, we will contact you at the email address used for the request.

Privacy Policy

For more information about how Intima handles data, see the Intima Privacy Policy.

Изтриване на акаунт и данни

За да изтриеш локалните данни, отвори Intima → Settings → Data → Delete everything. Това изтрива локалния дневник, календара, локалните медии, настройките и security keys от устройството.

За server-side изтриване изпрати имейл до dimitur.boychew@abv.bg със subject Intima account deletion request. Включи имейла за Google/Facebook вход, ако си използвал такъв, дали си използвал Partner Mode, и приблизителни дати на ползване, ако акаунтът е бил anonymous.

След проверка изтриваме или анонимизираме свързаните server-side auth records, Partner Mode записи, chat/media records, private diary media копия и Couple Match записи, където това е технически възможно. Някои backup/log данни може да останат временно за сигурност, troubleshooting, законови задължения или backup recovery.
